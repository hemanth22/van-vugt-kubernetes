Kubernetes problems may occur at different levels. In this scenario you will learn how to troubleshoot any Kubernetes problem in a structured way, while focussing on correct functioning of the Kubernetes nodes. 
 
In the following steps you will learn the following:
 
*	Applying troubleshooting techniques
*	Analysing application failure 
*	Troubleshooting kubelet related problems
